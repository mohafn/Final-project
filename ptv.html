Places to visit

Planning a trip by looking at these places that constitute a magnet for visitors to the Saudi.

Dir’iyah

​​​​​​​​​​​​The capital city of the first Saudi state and the Kingdom’s reflection mirroring power and pride, Dir’iyah is the city of resilience from which the modern Saudi state has emerged. It is distinguished for having diverse tourism components. Also, it is a World Heritage Site, and houses a number of spectacular buildings showcasing the ability of local engineers (Dir'iyah, n.d).

Mada’en Saleh

Mada’en Saleh, also known as Al Hijr, is a series sandstone outcrop of various sizes and heights surrounded by a ring sand mountains. It is a pre-Islamic archaeological site located in the Al-Ula sector, within the Al Madinah Region of Saudi Arabia. Mada’en Saleh is the most important settlement of the Nabataeans, second only to Petra. It’s most significant cultural role dates back to the first century, during the flourishing Nabataean state and before its fall at the hands of the Roman Empire in 106 (Alola & Mada'en Saleh, n.d).

Farasan Island

This spectacular island is blessed with a year-round moderate climate, variant weather and beautiful surroundings so travelers can relax and enjoy their visit. It can be enjoyed throughout the year given its temperate weather in both winter and summer, which is mainly due to it being surrounded by water on all sides, and the contrast of temperatures between land and water. Average temperatures in the summer reach 86 F, with humidity 66% in the summer (Farasan Island, n.d).

Asir City

Asir which is located in southwestern Saudi Arabia running parallel to the Red Sea, has a joyous outlook which is reflected in the local arts and handicrafts; vibrant hand-woven rugs and fabrics with distinctive geometric patterns, colourful beaded necklaces in coral and silver. Conditions are also ideal for hiking and mountain bike riding. And the Asir mountains provide the natural resources for some of the most beautiful campings in the World (Asir, n.d).

Al-Hada and AlShefa

​It is one of the most beautiful summer places in Saudi Arabia, which is linked with the best and latest mountain roads with neighboring cities. It offers all the services including a number of hotels and a large number of parks and gardens. Hada includes an agricultural area that produces apricots, figs, peaches, grapes and pomegranates in addition to roses that are produces in large quantities that are utilized in the extraction of essential oils and the rose water (Al-Hada, n.d).

Wadi Hanifah

This large park features natural landscapes, trees and a flowing water channel that offers numerous areas to enjoy recreation and the outdoors. Wadi Hanifah is the largest of its type, and one of the longest stretches of open recreational spaces reaching a length of 80 kilometers , from the north at Prince Salman Road to Hayer in the south.​​​
