General Information About Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

There will be some information about Sauid Arabia that will give you a simple idea what Saudi Arabia is.

Location

Saudi Arabia constitutes about four-fifths of the Arabian Peninsula in Southwest Asia. Saudi Arabia is bounded on the north by Jordan and Iraq, on the north east by Kuwait, on the east by the Arabian Gulf, Qatar, and the United Arab Emirates (UAE), on the south east by Oman, on the south by Yemen; and on the w by the Red Sea and the Gulf of Aqaba, with a total estimated land boundary length of 4,431 km (2,753 mi) and a coastline of 2,640 km (1,640 mi) (Saudi Arabia, n. d).

Government

The king of Saudi Arabia is Salman bin Abdulaziz.

Saudi Arabia is based monarchy in which the sovereign's dominant powers are regulated according to Muslim law. Constitution must be compatible with Islamic law. The Council of Ministers, first set up in 1953, is appointed by the king to advise on policy, originate legislation, and supervise the growing bureaucracy. The post of prime minister is reserved for the king and the crown prince is appointed first deputy prime minister. In 1992, King Fahd announced the creation of the Consultative Council, an advisory body that would provide a forum for public debate. The king appointed 60 citizens not belonging to the royal family to four-year terms on this body. In 2001, membership was increased to 120 (Saudi Arabia, n. d).

Climate

The climate is generally very dry and very hot. From May to September, the hottest period, daytime temperatures reach 54°c (129°f). From October through April, the climate is more moderate, with evening temperatures between 16° and 21°c (61° and 70°f). Average annual rainfall is 9 cm (3.5 in), with most rain falling from November to May (Saudi Arabia, n. d).

Capital City

Riyadh City

Riyadh is the capital and most populous city of Saudi Arabia. It is is located in the southern Najd region. It is also the capital of Riyadh Province and belongs to the historical regions of Najd and Al-Yamama. It is situated in the center of the Arabian Peninsula on a large plateau and home to more than six million people (Riyadh, n.d.).

Economy and Currenecy

The economy is heavily dependent on oil production, which provides approximately 45% of gross domestic product (GDP), 90% of export value, and 75% of government revenues (Saudi Arabia, n. d).

The Riyal is the currency of Saudi Arabia. It is abbreviated as SR (Saudi riyal). It is subdivided into 100 halalas (Saudi Arabia, n. d).

Religion and Language

Islam is the state religion. The holy city of Mecca is the center of Islam and the site of the sacred Ka'bah sanctuary, toward which all Muslims face at prayer (Saudi Arabia, n. d).

Arabic language is the formal language in the country. It is written in a cursive script from right to left. It has 28 letters of the alphabet (Saudi Arabia, n. d).
